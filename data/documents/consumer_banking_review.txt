MERIDIAN NATIONAL BANCORP -- CONSUMER BANKING SEGMENT REVIEW, FY 2023
=======================================================================

The Consumer Banking segment reported total revenue of $21.3 billion for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2023, an increase of 9.7% from $19.4 billion in FY 2022. Pre-tax income was $7.1 billion, up 12.4% year-over-year, driven by higher net interest income and disciplined expense management. The segment serves approximately 54.2 million consumer households across the Company's national footprint.

DEPOSITS
Average consumer deposits were $487 billion in FY 2023, up 6.1% from $459 billion. However, deposit mix continued to shift, with noninterest-bearing deposits declining to 26.3% of total consumer deposits from 31.7% in the prior year as customers moved balances into certificates of deposit and high-yield savings accounts. The average rate paid on consumer interest-bearing deposits rose 64 basis points to 2.18%.

MORTGAGE BANKING
Mortgage originations were $47.3 billion, a decline of 18.4% from $58.0 billion in FY 2022, as the 30-year fixed mortgage rate averaged 6.81% during the year. Purchase originations of $33.1 billion were down 9.2%, while refinance originations of $14.2 billion declined 35.8%. Mortgage banking revenue was $1.8 billion, down 22.6% from $2.3 billion, reflecting lower gain-on-sale margins and reduced volumes.

CREDIT CARDS
Credit card purchase volumes were $412 billion, up 8.3% year-over-year. Total card receivables were $92.4 billion, an increase of 7.8%. New card accounts opened during the year totaled 8.7 million. The net charge-off rate on the card portfolio was 2.94%, compared to 2.18% in FY 2022, reflecting a normalization from historically low loss rates. Card revenue, including interchange and finance charges, was $9.6 billion.

DIGITAL BANKING
Active digital banking users reached 41.8 million, up 7.2% from 39.0 million. Mobile banking users were 34.6 million, with 72.3% of all consumer transactions conducted through digital channels, compared to 67.8% in the prior year. Zelle transaction volume processed through the Company's platform was $198 billion, up 24.5%. Digital account openings represented 38.4% of new consumer checking accounts, up from 31.6%.
